About Us

Introduction

The Happy Clus is located in Ludhiana. The main objective of this club is to spread happiness and awareness in the current pandemic. We not only motivate people to survive in this current situation but also to help others.

At this Club, we help you connect to your true self so you can find inner happiness that lasts. Join us as we guide you on your journey of self-discovery through our easy, instant and affordable activities and online meditations designed to inspire positive change in your life.

Health, Safety and Environment are the key features of our Happy Club. Health represents salf care. Safety refers to stay safe and stay home in this covid-19 pandemic. Environment illustrate to conserve the natural resources and the natural environment to create Eco friendly surroundings.

This club play an important role in creating environmental awareness amongst the future generation. “Preserve” is our fundamental term and we give a lot of emphasis on it because the damage done to the Earth by now is way over the limit.
